Antioch University’s National Model for Mission-Driven Learning

For Changemakers Seeking More Than Just Flexibility

While many seek convenient online programs, Antioch students are building careers and lives around equity, sustainability, and systemic change. They want to advance their careers and improve the world around them.

What Graduate Programs Look Like at Antioch

Personalized

Students co-design learning pathways with faculty mentors, tailoring coursework to their values and communities.

Practical & Applied

Dissertations are tools for real-world change, not just academic exercises. The focus is on leading change, not just learning about it.

Purpose-Driven

Every program is anchored in a mission to advance social, environmental, and economic justice for the common good.

Social Justice: The Core of Every Program

Social justice isn't just an academic construct—it's a concrete component of the curriculum. Courses across all disciplines explicitly address inequality, power, privilege, and transformation, preparing graduates to create viable and equitable solutions.

Empowered Graduates, Measurable Impact

The success of Antioch’s programs is measured by what graduates do next. They are equipped with the skills to design inclusive, culturally responsive interventions that serve diverse populations.

80%

of Antioch graduate alumni go on to work in mission-driven organizations or civic leadership roles.
